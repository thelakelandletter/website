About Us

Welcome to The Lakeland Letter! We are a student led organization, dedicated to writing stories about what is important to us as Lakeland Students.

When we first started this newspaper, we really wanted to give updates on Lakeland happenings that are relevant to students, rather than force the same old updates over and over, never actually reporting on what matters to us as teenagers. We hope to write a few issues every year, and report on topics like sports, entertainment, general Lakeland activities, and the arts.

We are led by current junior Lucy Martin as founder and co-editor in chief alongside other current junior Katie Ginopolis.
